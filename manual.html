Master the reconnaissance workflow with NetPulse.

Setup Scanning Reporting Performance

1. Initial Setup

Launch the NetPulse application. The first time you run it, a history.json file will be created in your root directory to store your audit logs.

./NetPulse.exe --init

2. Targeted Scanning

Navigate to the IP Scanner tab. You can enter single IPs, ranges (e.g. 192.168.1.1-50), or CIDR blocks (e.g. 10.0.0.0/24).

Once targets are set, go to Port Scanner and select your desired profiles or individual ports.

3. Professional Reporting

After a scan completes, head to the Reports page. Here you can filter by port number (e.g. '22') or status ('Open') and preview the report before exporting to CSV or JSON.
