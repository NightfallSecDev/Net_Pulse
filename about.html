Behind the Pulse

The story of Crooksec and the evolution of NetPulse.

Who is Crooksec?

Crooksec is an independent security research collective focused on developing high-performance, low-level networking tools. We believe that security auditing should be fast, accessible, and powerful without the bloat of traditional enterprise software.

The NetPulse Vision

NetPulse was born out of a need for a reconnaissance suite that doesn't sacrifice speed for aesthetics. By building our own socket engine from the ground up, we achieved latencies that outperformed standard libraries, all while maintaining a premium interface that security researchers actually enjoy using.
